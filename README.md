📌 ABOUT FORMSENSE

FormSense is an intelligent AI assistant that transforms complex
government forms, scheme notifications, application guidelines,
and official documents into simple, easy-to-understand summaries.

Users can provide:
🌐 Government Website Links
📄 PDF Documents
📝 Raw Text Content

FormSense analyzes the content using AI and instantly highlights
the information that matters most.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

❓ THE PROBLEM

Government applications often contain:

🔹 Lengthy documents
🔹 Complex legal or technical language
🔹 Multiple eligibility conditions
🔹 Hidden deadlines
🔹 Numerous required certificates
🔹 Confusing instructions

As a result, many people struggle to understand whether they
qualify and what documents they need before applying.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

💡 THE SOLUTION

FormSense acts as your personal document interpreter.

Instead of reading dozens of pages, users receive a concise,
easy-to-read summary containing:

✅ What the form is about
✅ Who can apply
✅ Required documents
✅ Important deadlines
✅ Application process
✅ Simplified explanation
